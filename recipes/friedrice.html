Authentic Fried Rice

A beautiful dish filled with Egg,vegetables, and rice, that can add any protine you could desire.

  • Cooked, Chilled, Rice
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Carrots, Onions, Green onios and peas
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Toasted Sesame Oil
  • Butter

Perfectly Prepared Fired Rice

  1. Using a small pat of butter, Scramble some eggs in a large saute pan, breaking them into small pieces as you go. Then transfe the eggs to a separate plate and set aside.
  2. Saute your vegies and garlic
  3. Stir Fry the rice with the sauted veggies butter, soy sauce, oyster sauce, until the veggies start to brown.
  4. Remove the pan from heat and stir in your green onion
  5. Season to taste with salt, pepper, and any other spices you wish
  6. Add in any cooked protin of your choice and serve
